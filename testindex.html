About Josie's Seacamp Adventure

Join Josie on an exciting marine science adventure at Seacamp in Big Pine Key, FL! This trip is a unique opportunity for young people to study and live alongside wildlife in the Florida Keys. Through hands-on activities like snorkeling, SCUBA, and sailing, Josie will explore vibrant ecosystems and get closer than ever to marine life. As Seacamp describes, “Our unique program blends hands-on marine science with adventure, allowing campers to explore the Florida Keys’ ecosystems.” Your support helps make this unforgettable educational experience possible!
